About Us

Who Are We

Aster Care home is a licensed personal care home for seniors where comfort mingles with affordable budget. It is more than just a place to live. Our residents truly become part of our family. We always provide need base personalized care to our elderly citizen by treating them as if they were our own family. We know everyone’s needs are different, so our care plans are uniquely tailored to each individual. We emphasis on combined team effort. The team comprises management, our staff and most importantly the family of our resident. We are dedicated to provide the highest quality senior care with dignity, compassion and peace of mind for their families. We’re not just any place to live; we’re the perfect place to call home!

WHAT WE OFFER

Aster Care Services

Each resident has different needs and requires different levels of care. We do a comprehensive evaluation of each resident and follow a personalized care plan based upon their needs. Our caregivers devote the time and attention to get to know each resident’s habits, hobbies, personalities, daily routines, and favorite foods. In many cases, our staffs truly become our residents’ extended family. We recognize the role and input of family members and work with them closely in managing each resident’s health and well-being. Our Care Team undergoes extensive training to ensure that they can effectively carry out their duties and responsibilities. However, experience and training are not enough to be a part of our Care Team. We look for people that have a passion for caregiving. We offer a wide range of resident-focused services to meet your need.
